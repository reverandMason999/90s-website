Album Reviews

Repulsion: Horrified.

Genre: Grindcore.

Location: Flint, MI

Duration: 29:08

Description: What can I say??? An absolute masterpiece, you can't get much more grimy than Repulsion and this album speaks to that.

The album was released by Necrosis records October of 1989.

Emperor: In the Nightside Eclipse.

Genre: Symphonic Black Metal.

Location: Notodden, Norway.

Duration: 48:44

Description: Yet another classic brought by of Norway's premier Black metal act, this album provides a cold frostbitten sound laden with ferocious riffing by the guitarist "Samoth". This album paved the way for what would become Symphonic Black Metal.

This album was released by Candlelight records July of 1994

Incantation: Onward to Golgotha

Genre: Death Metal

Location: Johnstown, PA

Duration: 45:26

Description: Here's one for fans of skull crushing Death Metal, the Pennsylvania four piece has proved time and time again that they are one of the most inventive bands in the scene.

This album was released in January of 1992 by Relapse Records.

Carcass: Heartwork

Genre: Death Metal/Grindcore

Location: Liverpool, UK

Duration: 41:52

Description: This album is an absolute banger! Although a huge change of pace from their earlier more grindcore oriented work, Heartwork is a complex masterpiece combining Death Metal, Grindcore and Rock.

This album was released in October of 1993 by Earache Records.

Dissection: The Somberlain

Genre: Death/Black Metal

Location: Stockholm, Sweden

Description: Summoning forces far beyond this realm of existence, Dissection comes through with of the coldest emotional dislays of its respective genre I have personally ever heard.

This album was released on the record label No Fashion in December 1993.
